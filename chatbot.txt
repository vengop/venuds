NIT Warangal is conducting a course in Applied Artificial Intelligence program 

The course start on June 22 nd and it is a 16 weekend program .

The fee has two step process 

Registration and Application : You need to pay Rs 5000 to register for the course
your application will be screened  and your application fee will be refunded in case you
application is not approved, if it is approved , then you are entitled to attend preparatory course 

Enrollment : Here you pay Rs 85000 plus taxes to enroll and start the course.

the course syllabus has Predictive Analytics,machine learning and artificial intelligence as modules
each module is again divided into units , please check nitwai.com for more details.
